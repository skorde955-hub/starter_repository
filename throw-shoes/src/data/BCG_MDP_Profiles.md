
# BCG Managing Directors & Partners — Profiles

## 1. Varun Kejriwal
Varun Kejriwal is a Managing Director & Partner at BCG in Mumbai, specialising in the People & Organization practice and the Financial Institutions sector.  
He focuses on digital performance management, organisation design, leadership development and end‑to‑end customer journeys in the context of banking and public sector enterprises.  
He also leads the “Seat‑At‑The‑Table” (SATT) agenda and marketing for the People & Organization practice in the Asia‑Pacific region.  
**Education:** MBA from XLRI Jamshedpur and BE in Computer Science from BIT.  
He co‑authored BCG’s annual FIBAC productivity report on Indian banking, reflecting his role in shaping industry benchmarks.  

---

## 2. Rahul Sanghvi
Rahul Sanghvi is a Managing Director & Partner at BCG’s Mumbai office.  
He is a core member of BCG’s Industrial Goods and Travel, Cities & Infrastructure practices, with deep expertise in building materials, metals & chemicals, logistics and digital transformation.  
He works with CEOs and management teams to drive large‑scale business transformations across India, the Netherlands and the UK.  
**Education:** MBA from XLRI Jamshedpur and BCom (Hons.) from Shri Ram College of Commerce, Delhi University.  

---

## 3. Saurabh Verma
Saurabh Verma is a Managing Director & Partner at BCG, India lead for the BCG Transform practice, and a core member of the Industrial Goods practice.  
He brings over 14 years of consulting experience in industries such as steel, automotive, metals & mining, and chemicals across India, SEA, Africa, the UK and North America.  
His specialty is driving bold multi‑year transformations—such as digital‑led operations, large capex programmes, value acceleration and organisational professionalisation of family businesses.  

---

## 4. Pallavi Malani
Pallavi Malani is a Managing Director & Partner at BCG based in India, leading the Insurance Practice in India and the Digital Growth Acceleration practice for the Asia Pacific region.  
She holds an MBA in Strategy and Marketing from ISB and a BA (Honors) in Economics from St Stephen’s College, Delhi.  
With over 13 years of consulting experience at BCG, she helps insurers, financial institutions and tech companies drive digital transformation and customer‑centric innovation.  
She has co‑authored industry reports like *“India Insurtech Landscape and Trends: Pathways to Inclusive Insurance – A Vision for 2047.”*
